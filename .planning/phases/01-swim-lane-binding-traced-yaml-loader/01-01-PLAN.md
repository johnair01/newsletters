## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|

| T-01-02 | Denial of Service | `_yaml_loader` parse of malformed YAML | accept | `safe_load` raises `yaml.YAMLError` on malformed input; the loader surfaces it (never swallows). Fixture-sized inputs only; no untrusted-scale exposure. |

It is a canonical, non-AI, spec-named package — **not** `[ASSUMED]`/`[SUS]`, so no blocking-human legitimacy checkpoint is required. Confined behind the `[config]` extra + lazy boundary. |
